# Repository Quality Scoring System

## 11 Factors (100 points total)

1. **Stars** (0-30) - Logarithmic scale
2. **Forks** (0-15) - Logarithmic scale  
3. **Description** (0-10) - Quality matters
4. **Topics** (0-10) - 2 points each
5. **Size** (0-15) - Complexity indicator
6. **Homepage** (0-5) - Has demo/site
7. **Recent Updates** (0-10) - Active maintenance
8. **License** (0-5) - Professional
9. **Language** (0-10) - Popular languages
10. **Watchers** (0-5) - Ongoing interest
11. **Issues** (0-5) - Active usage

## Why This Matters

**For 0-star repos:** Can still score 50-70 points with good practices!

**Example:**
- 0 stars, but good description, 5 topics, 2MB size, updated recently, has license, TypeScript
- Score: 65/100 → Ranks in top 5! ✅

## Quick Wins

Add these for instant 30 points:
- Good description (10 pts)
- 5 topics (10 pts)
- License (5 pts)
- Homepage (5 pts)

Your quality work gets recognized, not just popularity! 🌟
